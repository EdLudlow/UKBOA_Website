About:

Have you wondered if Brad Pitt is as popular as he was 10 years ago? Or if Joker made as much Money as Frozen 2? Or who has appeared in the most films in the UK since 2015? UKBOA might be able to help...


UK Box Office Analytics (UKBOA) is an interactive data visualization tool which tracks the performance of films, actors, directors and screenwriters at the UK box office since 2001. Using the BFI's weekly box office reports, UKBOA gauges how films, and the people involed in making them have seen their financial returns fluctuate over time, providing an insight into the changing tastes of the British cinema-going public.

While it should be noted that UKBOA does not use an exhaustive data-set (the source only includes the weekly box office for the top 15 films, British films, and new releases), the tool provides a helpful insight into the general trends of UK box office performance.


Ed Ludlow:

UKBOA was conceived, created and designed by Ed Ludlow, a film publicist who lives and works in London.
