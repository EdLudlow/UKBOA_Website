UK Box Office Analytics (UKBOA) provides interactive data visualization for the performance of films, actors, directors and screenwriters at the UK box office since 2001. Using the BFI's weekly box office reports, UKBOA tracks whether a film, or the people who were involved in making it, have seen their returns increase, decrease, or roughly stay the same over time.
